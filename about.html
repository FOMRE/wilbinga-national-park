The Vision

To establish Wilbinga National Park as a permanent northern green boundary for Perth, protecting biodiversity and cultural heritage for future generations.

Location

Bounded by the Indian Ocean to the west, Indian Ocean Drive to the east, Two Rocks to the south and the Moore River Estuary to the north.

Why Unique?

  • Within Southwest Australia Biodiversity Hotspot
  • Five times the size of Kings Park
  • Coastal limestone ecosystem
  • Close to metropolitan Perth
