A Once-in-a-Generation Opportunity

Wilbinga National Park represents a rare opportunity to permanently protect coastal bushland at Perth’s northern boundary between Two Rocks and the Moore River Estuary.

80% of the proposed area is already zoned Conservation or Recreation.

Establishing Wilbinga as a National Park would safeguard biodiversity, groundwater systems, Indigenous heritage and provide sustainable tourism and recreation opportunities.
